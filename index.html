Average Annual Temperature on Earth
through recorded history

In 1850 Earth's average temperature was 13.7°c and 56.7°f .  

In 1900 Earth's average temperature was 13.5°c and 56.4°f - a change of -1.0% from 1850.

In 1950 Earth's average temperature was 13.8°c and 56.8°f - a change of +1.8% from 1900.

In 2000 Earth's average temperature was 14.5°c and 58.1°f - a change of +4.9% from 1950.

In 2014 Earth's average temperature was 15.1°c and 59.1°f - a change of +3.8% from 2000.

In 2024 Earth's average temperature was 15.7°c and 60.3°f - a change of +4.2% from 2014.

 

Over the last 175 years Earth's average temperature has changed +2.0°c and +3.6°f .

 

In 2050 Earth's average temperature is estimated to be 16.9°c and 62.4°f .  

In 2075 Earth's average temperature is estimated to be 18.1°c and 64.6°f .
